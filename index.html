About TED

TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages. Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world.

About TEDx

TEDx is an independently organized TED event which helps communities enjoy a TED like experience, through live speakers and videos of TED talks. Extensively conducted by leading universities and organizations, TEDx events have seen incredible growth across the globe. A TEDx event aims to make great ideas accessible and spark conversation among the members of the audience. Idea-centric, crisp, 18-minute deliverances by eminent personalities from diverse fields inspire and educate alike. The breaks provide ample time to strike up conversations with other enthusiasts, paving the way for interesting discussions and varied perspectives.

About TEDxVSSUT

The first edition of TEDx was launched in 2018 after a series of setbacks. Where in one hand we have the unrivalled concept of TED influencing thousands of people every day, we have people who do it for changing people's lives with nothing expected in return. Those who do it for passion and are skilled in the craft always do it better. TEDx VSSUT is a perfect paradigm of how big events like TEDx can be modelled and organised in areas like Burla. The first edition of TEDx was launched under the theme of “SOLVING THE UNSOLVED”. 15 speakers were invited from across all walks of life and a grand event was organized along with an introductory Gala Dinner. TEDx VSSUT is back again to organise its second edition here which promises to be only better and improve on the benchmark. Since then, the team has gained more experience and is committed to work through the past flaws & improve on it relentlessly.

F.A.Q

  • What is TEDxVSSUT?

    TEDxVSSUT is a TEDx event being conducted in VSSUT, Burla, one of the premier engineering institute of the state. It is an attempt to bring TEDx to Western Odisha and provide people with an enriching experience to witness eminent personalities spreading their ideas. TEDxVSSUT started in the year 2018 under the theme “SOLVING THE UNSOLVED”. Its second edition is to be held under the theme “FAST.FORWARD.FUTURE” in 2019.

  • What is the relevance of the theme?

    The theme serves as the bedrock of the event based on which speakers and their topics are selected. The first edition in 2018 focussed on problem-solving. The second edition, however, revolves around visionaries and how they are shaping the future in their respective domains.

  • How can I attend the TEDx event?

    The event is ticketed and thus anyone with an entry ticket will be able to attend the event. Tickets will be available from (date). A limited number of seats will be available and the tickets will be sold online on (page). The price will be mentioned in the registration window.

  • How can I know when the ticket sales begin?

    To know the date of ticket sale, drop your email at (link) and you will be informed as soon as the tickets are made available.

  • What is the last date for registration?

    There is no definite date since the window will be open untill the maximum allowed seats are filled. The counter on the registration page will show the number of seats left. However, to ensure a seat, it is advisable to book the seat as soon as possible.

  • What are the transportation facilities available to Burla?

    Transportation facilities to Burla includes Bus and Train. The nearest station is at Hirakud situated at a distance of 2.2 km. Bus facility is available from other states to Sambalpur. The Sambalpur Bus Terminal is situated at a distance of 9 km from the University.

    The event is scheduled to start on 10 AM and continue till 4 PM. Full detailed schedule will be uploaded on the website shortly.
